Philleese’s Secret Photo Site

 
 

Do’s


  1. •    Feel free to download photos — of yourself — for posting on your refrigerator, or your private facebook or myspace page, etc.

  2. •    Respect others' privacy and realize that not everyone shares your sense of humor.

  3. •    Read the manifesto so you understand what we're trying to do.

Don'ts


  1. •    Do not post unflattering photos of yourself — or anyone else — in a publicly-accessible place on the internet, ever.

  2. •    Do not copy photos of people other than yourself or close friends from these pages.

  3. •    Do not post links to any of these pages in a public place.

Please follow these Do’s and Don’ts

Thanks for your cooperation!

How to Download Photos: 

  1. 1.    Click on Contact Sheets, Highlights, or Archived Highlights to see the photo albums

  2. 2.    Click on one of the photo albums

  3. 3.    Click on the photo you want to download - to see a large version of it

  4. 4.    Click on the Download link in the upper right corner to see just the photo, all by itself

  5. 5.    Right-click on the photo and choose the option to  "Save Image" or “Save Image As...” to save it on your PC

  6. 6.    For best results create a new folder for these photos, naming it for example "club photos" or “nightlife”

  7. 7.    Also consider renaming the photo, adding for example the date, who is in it, or where it was taken to the file name, so you can know what it is a photo of without having to look at the image

  8. 8.    Remember to be careful of which photos you download and where you post them - we don’t want anyone getting fired just for having a little fun on the weekend!

  9. 9.    Click your browser’s Back button to return to the site
